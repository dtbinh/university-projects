Projects: Assessment

Each project will be assessed based on the following factors:
  • 15 points: Completeness and quality of the project report.
  • 15 points: Completeness of the test procedures.
  • 50 points: Correct operation of the client and server processes.
  • 10 points: Implementation of optional features.
  • 10 points: IETF Draft/RFC Compliance.
